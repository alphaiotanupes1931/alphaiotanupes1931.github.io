Chapter Updates

Posted by: Godley Pierre, Polemarch June 5, 2024

Summer Programming Plans

Brothers, I hope this message finds you well as we enter the summer season. We have several important initiatives planned for the coming months:

1. The Summer Leadership Retreat is confirmed for July 22-24 at the Morgan State Alumni House. Please mark your calendars and plan to attend.

2. Our community service program with Baltimore Youth Connect will continue throughout the summer. We need at least 5 brothers per session, so please sign up on the volunteer spreadsheet.

3. Congratulations to our newly initiated brothers! Your energy and enthusiasm have already made a positive impact on our chapter.

Posted by: Xavier Johnson, Vice Polemarch June 1, 2024

Province Meeting Report

Brothers, I'm pleased to report that our chapter had an excellent showing at the recent Province Meeting. We received recognition for our academic achievement and community service programs.

Special congratulations to Brothers Terell Reed and Marcus McClean for their awards in leadership excellence. The Province Polemarch specifically mentioned our chapter's mentorship program as a model for other chapters to follow.

The full meeting minutes and presentation slides have been uploaded to our shared drive. Please review them before our next chapter meeting.
