Polemarch's Welcome

Greetings to all who have come to learn more about "THE NOTORIOUS" Alpha Iota Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. As the current Polemarch, I am honored to welcome you to our beloved chapter.


Our brothers are trailblazers, innovators, and visionaries who have made indelible marks in their respective fields. From Grand Chapter Officers to Province Leaders, the Alpha Iota Chapter continues to embody the ideals of Kappa Alpha Psi and inspire the next generation of change-makers.
